Sports Jigsaw Activity

In this activity you will work in a group of five students. Each member of the group will be given one sport. You will research this sport and become an "expert" on it. After researching, you will present your research to your group and teach them about your assigned sport.

Directions:

  1. Get together with your pre-assigned group of five students.
  2. Each member of the group will be assigned a number from 1 to 5. Look at the table below to see what sport corresponds with your number.
  3. Use the 3 links provided to study your topic and become an "expert" on it.
  4. Join a new group with students that have the same sport as you. Discuss your findings on this sport and answer any questions anyone might have.
  5. Finally, return to your original group. Each student will get a chance to teach the group about the sport that they researched.
  6. Once all groups are finished, the class will have a quiz on all five sports assigned.
